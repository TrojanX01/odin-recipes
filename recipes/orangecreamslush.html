Orange Cream Slush

Add some vanilla-flavored vodka, and you've got yourself a delicious icy creamsicle.

This is a simple and delicious recipe for a cool and creamy orange drink.

Ingredients

You will be needing:

  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 8 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Combine the orange juice, milk, sugar, and ice cubes in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
