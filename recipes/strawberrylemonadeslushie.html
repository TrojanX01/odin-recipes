Strawberry Lemonade Slushie

Only 4 ingredients and a cinch to make a summer drink that is sure to represh refresh and delight.

You're four ingredients and a few pulses in a blender away from a refreshing slushie. As is, it's kid-friendly, but adults could add some vodka or tequila for a boozy kick.

Ingredients

You will be needing:

  • Â½ cup water
  • cup dry lemonade-flavored drink mix
  • 3 cups ice cubes
  • 1 cup strawberries

Steps

  1. Stir water and lemonade mix together in a cup until the mix dissolves.
  2. Combine ice cubes and strawberries in a blender.
  3. Pour lemonade mixture into the blender.
  4. Blend on high for 10 seconds.
  5. Stop blender and stir with a spoon.
  6. Blend again until smooth, about 5 seconds more.
