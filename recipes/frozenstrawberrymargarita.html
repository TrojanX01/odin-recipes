Frozen Strawberry Margarita

We serve these when friends come over and they are always sure to liven up a party!

Pop all five ingredients into a blender and you're done. This is the perfect party drink for an impromptu gathering.

Ingredients

You will be needing:

  • 6 cups ice
  • 4 fluid ounces frozen limeade concentrate
  • 8 ounces frozen strawberries
  • 6 fluid ounces tequila
  • 2 fluid ounces triple sec

Steps

  1. Place ice in a blender and crush for 15 to 20 seconds.
  2. Add frozen strawberries, tequila, triple sec, and limeade.
  3. Blend until smooth.
